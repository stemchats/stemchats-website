About Us

STEMchats aims to democratize STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math) education for all by bringing impactful STEM programming to all students, especially disadvantaged students. We are guided by our core values: inclusiveness, collaboration, encouragement, and laidback.

We emphasize these values through both our workplace culture as well as our programs, which are geared towards empowering disadvantaged students to use the power of STEM for social good.
