Business Development

The Business Development teams work to help maintain our organization, support our 5 STEMchats programs, and connect our work with the public.

External Development

The External Development Team collaborates with all the STEMchats teams to handle any external initiatives to progress public-facing aspects of STEMchats in addition to solidifying our place in the STEM, education, nonprofit, and social impact spaces. This includes helping plan events (virtual or in-person), conducting partnership outreach and maintaining the partnership database, and any other various external initiatives.

Currently, External Development is spearheading the creation of a STEMchats Slack community, conducting partnership and promotional outreach for our virtual events, helping to manage the STEMchats University Chapters Initiative, outlining methods to make our platforms and programming more accessible, helping to run an upcoming STEMchats policy initiative, and working with the Marketing Team on the new STEMchats Youtube channel.

This work is critical to the success of STEMchats since a majority of our impact occurs through public-facing programming and initiatives.

Internal Development

The Internal Development Team works on making sure everything runs smoothly within STEMchats. As a nonprofit organization that aims to have a large impact on the STEM field, we have to make sure all the teams work efficiently while maintaining a strong team culture.

This is achieved through identifying Key Performance Metrics (KPIs) and then working around these to submit internal audit reports, which include recommendations for the numerous teams.

Other projects include team socials, assisting the Leadership Team with onboarding and recruitment, member evaluations, and undertaking various new initiatives, such as the Internal Newsletter and Professional and Personal Development Program for our members.

Funding

The Funding Team focuses on the financial aspects of STEMchats to ensure that all programs and initiatives run successfully without having to endure any financial burdens - much like our mission to provide equitable education at no cost!

The Funding team stays in constant communication with all five programs to prepare accordingly by creating budget lists, researching applicable grants, reaching out to companies/organizations for sponsorships, brainstorming alternative fundraising methods, and assisting with donations and donor relations.

While the main objective is to obtain money, Funding works with both the Marketing and External Development teams to build a lasting relationship with our sponsors/partners, reach and captivate new and existing audiences, and ultimately, establish a financially sustainable nonprofit to accomplish our mission!

Marketing
